SAVEOTTERS
Endangered Status
Sea otters face decreasing populations due to environmental issues like oil spills, commericial fishing, and lack of prey.
Friendly creatures

Sea otters behave friendly towards people (unless provoked!) and are intelligient and curious beings.

How are sea otters being protected?

Many efforts like the International Fur Seal Trade protect sea otters from being hunted. The best thing you can do is be environmentally conscious and donate to conservation groups.
